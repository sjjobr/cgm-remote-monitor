How Loop + Omnipod Dash works

Loop reads your Dexcom G7 every 5 minutes and predicts BG 6 hours ahead. When high, it tells the Omnipod Dash to increase its temp basal rate. The Dash has no onboard algorithm — Loop is entirely in charge. A manual correction is only needed when Loop is paused or out of Bluetooth range.

Suspend threshold: 65 mg/dL — Loop stops all insulin below this level.
